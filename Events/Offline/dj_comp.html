  • Event Description
This event is a thrilling showcase of the most talented and creative DJs in the area. It's a night of music, excitement, and friendly competition as each DJ takes the stage to demonstrate their skills and unique style.

Rules:
  • Each participant will be allowed a total time of 25 mins, including 5 min of set up time.
  • Minimum of 7 tracks should be played.
  • Pre mixed sets and Bollywood mashups will not be allowed.
  • Only original tracks are allowed.
  • No genre limitations.
  • Contestants should bring their own CDs/pen drives & headphones.
  • Standard pioneer CDJs and DJM will be provided.
  • Laptops/DJ performance software will not be allowed during the time of performance.
  • Marks given based on number of tracks mixed, scratching, crowd control, emceeing, creating FX usage, C mixing skills, live mashups, acapella mixing and professional approach.
  • If the number of participants exceeds a certain limit prelim will be conducted.
  • First prize shall be declared alone in case of 3 participating teams.
  • Decision of judges will be final and binding under all circumstances.
  • Each participant should carry their respective college/university identity proof.
  • Every participant must be a college/university student.
