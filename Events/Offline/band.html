  • Event Description
When you create a LIFESTYLE photographs the main goal is to tell a story through candid, unstaged scenes. Usually, photographers capture simple things and real-life situations to create moody and authentic visuals. When making lifestyle portraits, direct the action, not the poses. Ask your models to run, dance, jump, or spin, and be ready to capture the best shot.

Rules:
  • Avoid duplicate entries.
  • Entries from any digital cameras, including smartphones will be accepted.
  • Basic editing, including colour enhancement, the use of filters and cropping of photos without affecting the authenticity and genuineness are acceptable.
  • There must be no borders, logos, copyright marks, identifying marks, or any other visible references or marks on image.
  • Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions or manipulations and the adding and removing of significant elements within the frame is PROHIBITED.
  • Photography must be in digital format and should not exceed 15MB.
  • Winners will be selected on the basis of quality and creativity of photographs.
  • Winners will be announced based on 50% like and share and 50% on judge’s decision.
  • Auto likes leads to disqualification.
  • Judges’ decision will be final.
  • Likes will be counted until 12pm on April 22nd.
  • The results will be announced on Swastika main stage.
