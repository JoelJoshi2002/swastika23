About Swastika

Swastika - The National Level Techno-Cultural Fest of Mar Baselios Christian College of Engineering and Technology, Peermade.Swastika offers a unique platform for the participants from across the country to compete and showcase their technical skills, along with the celebration of their talents. With a target audience belonging to different Engineering Streams and various geo-cultural locales, the event caters the exchange of innovative thoughts and brilliant minds. The Fest is, thus, one of its kinds in this part of the country attracting creative intellects and Engineering alike.
