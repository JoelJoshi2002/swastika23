Mari Ferrari

  • Event Description
Marri Ferrari is an internationally renowned DJ and producer known for her high-energy performances and unique blend of electronic dance music. With hit tracks like "Shameless" and "Not Yours", she has become a sensation in the EDM world. At the Pro show, Marri Ferrari will be performing a live DJ set that is sure to get the crowd moving and dancing.

The Pro show will feature state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems that will enhance the overall experience. The event will also include live visuals, special effects, and other surprises that will make the performance even more unforgettable. We hope you will join us for this exciting event and enjoy an unforgettable night of music and entertainment.

Guidelines:

1. College ID Card: A valid college ID card is mandatory for registration, and all college students are eligible to register for the event. Students should carry their valid college ID card at all times during the event.
2. Registration Fee: The first 50 participants who register for the Pro show will have no registration fee. After the first 50 registrations, a fee of Rs. 150 will be charged for registration.
3. Prohibited Items: The following items are strictly prohibited at the event: weapons of any kind, outside food or beverages, glass containers, laser pointers, illegal substances, and any other item deemed inappropriate by the event organizers.
4. Photography and Videography: Photography and videography will be allowed during the event, but the use of professional equipment such as tripods and large cameras is strictly prohibited.
5. Timing: The Pro show will begin promptly at the scheduled time, and attendees are advised to arrive at least 30 minutes prior to the start of the event to ensure a smooth check-in process. Latecomers may not be admitted into the venue.
6. Security: The event will be monitored by security personnel, and any disruptive behaviour will not be tolerated. Any issues or quarrels will be handled by the police directly.

We hope you find this information helpful and look forward to welcoming you to the Pro show. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
